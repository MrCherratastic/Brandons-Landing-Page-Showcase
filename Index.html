    • New Cars With 'Rolled Tips'

      New for 2022 cars the rear wing creates a rotational airflow that collects the rear wheel wake and rolls it into the flow exiting the diffuser. All that to say cars following behind don't have as much wind resistence and can stay closer forlonger and pass easier which make racing better for the fans.

    • Pirelli Hits The Asphalt

      F1's fan base has layed their eyes on a new 18-inch set of low profile tyres provided by Pirelli. The goal of these new Pirelli tyres is to reduce the tyre material heating up excessivly when sliding in and out corners. In return the end result should be much more wheel to wheel action.

    For The Love Of Cars

    The new era of F1 begins with some much anticipated changes in the garage.

    A Decrease In Downforce

    2022 now introduces "clean air" the after affect of "catastrophic downforce loss". This means driverw behind the lead car can stay closer for longer and close the gap between pole positions.

    A Decrease In Numbers

    2021 cars measured downforce loss of 46% at 10m distance. As For 2022, thoose numbers were reduced to 18% at 10m distance. Let the races begin!
